General

What is Haiphen in one sentence? general
Haiphen is a signals-intelligence engineering studio: we build inspectable, production-grade pipelines for ML/NLP, security, and telemetry-heavy systems — including the trading telemetry you see on our platform.
What does "API Everything" actually mean here? general
It means every meaningful output is addressable: metrics are published as JSON, screenshots are generated deterministically, and internal steps emit traceable intermediate state. The UI is basically a viewer for those artifacts.

Services & Delivery

What deliverables do you actually ship? services
Typically: (1) a working pipeline/service, (2) published artifacts (JSON, exports, screenshots), (3) observability (logs/metrics/traces) + runbooks, (4) reliability hardening (retries/idempotency/backfills), and (5) security boundaries (least privilege, key rotation posture).
What does a "thin vertical slice" mean in practice? engineering
One signal, one decision, one output. Example: ingest a single source, compute a KPI, publish JSON, render one UI widget, and add monitoring. Once it's reliable, scale horizontally (more signals).
How do you define success for a project? services
Success is measurable: latency/throughput targets, error budgets, alert coverage, correctness checks, and the ability to explain any output (lineage). If you can't debug it, you don't own it.
Can you integrate with our existing stack? services
Yes — we usually meet you where you are: your cloud, your CI, your observability, your auth. The goal is to reduce operational friction, not add another mystery system.

Security & Data

How do you handle sensitive data? security
We design for least privilege and separation of concerns: scoped tokens, minimized retention, audit logs, and clear data boundaries. If needed we can keep raw data in your environment and only publish derived artifacts.
Are the trading metrics investment advice? disclaimer
No. They're operational telemetry from an automated execution and risk system (volume, stability, daily outcomes). Nothing on this site is a recommendation to buy/sell any asset.

Cloud-Native & Deployment

What is Haiphen's deployment model? cloud-native
Haiphen runs entirely on Cloudflare Workers — a serverless edge compute platform with 200+ global PoPs. There are no VMs, containers, or infrastructure to manage. Your requests are processed at the nearest edge location with sub-millisecond cold starts.
Can I self-host Haiphen? cloud-native
Currently Haiphen is offered as a managed cloud service only. The CLI (haiphen serve) runs a local gateway on your machine that proxies authenticated requests to our edge infrastructure. This gives you a local endpoint while we handle reliability, scaling, and security.
What infrastructure do I need to use Haiphen? cloud-native
None. Install the CLI via Homebrew (brew install haiphenAI/tap/haiphen), Docker, or call the API directly. Authentication is handled via GitHub OAuth. There are no databases, queues, or servers to provision on your end.
What are the benefits of Haiphen's cloud-native architecture? cloud-native
  • Zero ops overhead — no servers, containers, or infrastructure to manage
  • Global low-latency — requests served from 200+ Cloudflare edge PoPs
  • Automatic scaling — no capacity planning or autoscaler tuning
  • Built-in security — DDoS protection, TLS termination, and WAF included
  • Cost efficiency — pay only for compute used, no idle resource costs
