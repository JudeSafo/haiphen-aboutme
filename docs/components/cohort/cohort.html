Program Overview

What is the cohort?

A time‑boxed onboarding and enablement period. It provides structured access to Haiphen’s platform, APIs, datasets, documentation, and community.

What problem does it solve?

Reduces integration friction and accelerates time‑to‑signal by pairing direct access with guided onboarding and QA.

Who is it for?

Professionals evaluating quantitative signal delivery, fintech infrastructure, or trading‑adjacent automation workflows.

FAQ

Is this managed trading or consulting?

No. The cohort is enablement only — not managed services or outcome‑based delivery.

How long is the cohort?

Three (3) months, per the service agreement.

What are the SLA targets?

Community responses within 24–72 business hours. Critical issues within 12 business hours.

Are fees refundable once the cohort begins?

Fees are non‑refundable once a cohort begins unless explicitly stated otherwise.

Are results guaranteed?

No. Services are provided “as‑is” with no guarantees of performance or revenue.
